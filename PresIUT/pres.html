Presentation of the UIT

The University Institution of Technology is a foundation in the higher educations where students can learn technologic knowledge and obtain a diploma that will allow students to find a job or integrate an engineering school where they can obtain an engineering degree.

The UIT has 565 computers dedicated to learning, 50 classrooms, 1 factory possessing several robotic materials and several industiral managing softwares.
To help students in their studies more than 38 professors and reaserchers are ready to help.
Last year the UIT had 680 students working to achieve their goals.

Associations and surroundings of the students

The UIT has several associations, one for each diploma (ACGO,INFO,INFOCOM...)
Each of the associations has their own club where everyone is free to join. Ranging from tabletop games to all sorts of sports
The students also have several activities and places to go outside of the university, such as supermarkets, bakeries, sport centers, etc...

Diplomas that the UIT can help you obtain

BUTs (equivalent to a university degree) :

  • GACO (Corporate and Administrative Management)
  • INFOCOM (Information and Communication)
  • Informatique (Computer science)
  • QLIO (Quality industrial logistics and organisation)

And professional degrees.
